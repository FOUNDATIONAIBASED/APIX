ℹ️

        
Messages are sent from personal SIM numbers. The delay engine mimics natural human sending to avoid carrier flags.

      

    


    
    

      
Choose Sender Numbers

      
Select which SIM numbers from your connected devices will send this campaign. Numbers are fetched live from approved devices.

      
Distribution Strategy

      

        
🔄 Round Robin
Rotate evenly across all selected numbers. Best equal carrier representation.

        
⏱ Least Recently Used
Always pick the number that sent least recently.

        
📊 Least Used Today
Balance daily send counts. Prevents any single number appearing over-active.

        
🎲 Random
Randomise sender selection — most natural-looking pattern.
